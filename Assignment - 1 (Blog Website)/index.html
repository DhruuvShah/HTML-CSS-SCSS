Mornings, Momentum & My Favourite Anime

By Dhruv · 6 min read · Routine & Anime

Some habits quietly shape the person you become — and for me, mornings are exactly that. This post is a small window into my daily routine, and into the anime worlds that keep my evenings just as alive.

My day starts before the sun is properly up, somewhere around 4 AM. After splashing some water on my face, I ease into a short stretch-and-warm-up routine before heading straight to the gym. That hour isn't just about training — it's where I find my focus, my discipline, and honestly, my favourite part of the entire day.
