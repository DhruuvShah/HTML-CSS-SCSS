The person behind Sparknotes

Equal parts gym regular, anime enthusiast, and self-appointed food critic. Here's a little more about who I am and why I bother writing any of this down.

Hi, I'm Dhruv

By day I'm chasing personal bests at the gym; by night, I'm usually a few episodes deep into something with a soundtrack that's far too good for 2 AM. Sparknotes started as a place to put those small, ordinary moments into words — the early mornings, the ever-growing watchlist I swear I'll finish, and the food I will absolutely not stop talking about.

Why this blog exists

I started writing because I wanted a record of the small things — the routines that quietly shape a day, the shows that stay with you long after the credits roll, and the meals worth pausing a conversation for. If even one post makes you smile, nod along, or add something new to your watchlist or your plate, I'll happily call that a win.
