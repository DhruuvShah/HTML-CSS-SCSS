How we work

Three habits we've never broken

Good coffee isn't a single decision — it's a hundred small, repeatable ones. Here are the three that matter most to us.

  • 01 / Source

    Direct trade, every season

    We buy straight from three family farms in Ethiopia, Colombia and Sumatra, and pay well above fair-trade rates — no brokers in between.

  • 02 / Roast

    Drum-roasted in-house

    Every batch goes through our 1958 Probat drum roaster, then rests for ten days before it's ground — long enough for the flavour to settle.

  • 03 / Pour

    Cupped before it's served

    Our baristas blind-taste every new batch before it goes on the menu. If the table doesn't agree on it, it doesn't make the cut.
