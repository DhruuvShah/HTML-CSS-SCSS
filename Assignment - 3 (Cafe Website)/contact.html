Before you come by

A few things worth knowing

  • Seating

    Six seats, first come

    We don't take reservations — the counter seats six, and there's a small bench outside for when the weather's kind.

  • Beans to go

    Take the roast home

    Every coffee on the menu is also sold by the 250g bag, with the roast date printed on the label.

  • Roastery visits

    Come watch a batch roast

    We open the roastery to visitors on Saturday mornings — write to us and we'll save you a spot at the cupping table.
